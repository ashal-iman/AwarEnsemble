Our mission is to empower foster youth transitioning to university life.

Who We Are

AWARENSEMBLE is a community driven resource platform built to support youth from foster care systems, especially those entering post-secondary education. We understand that this transition can be overwhelming, which is why we aim to simplify access to housing, mental health care, education aid, and life skills tools.

Our Goals

  • Make support services easy to find and understand
  • Reduce stigma and isolation for youth from care
  • Empower independence, safety, and wellness
  • Foster a sense of belonging and advocacy

How You Can Help

If you're an ally, mentor, educator, or organization, we welcome your input and collaboration. Please reach out so we can continue expanding the toolkit.
